As a part of the IEEE Day 2023, IEEE SB GVPCE(A) is here with an intresting concept which includes the concept of Housie game but there's a twist! It includes mathematics as well that will push the boundaries and enhance your inner mathematician like never before 👩‍🔬📐

Competition Rules:

    It comprises of 2 rounds✨

  • ➗Round 1: It includes a story narration, the story includes few mathametical digits within it and you have to perform some mathematical operations using BODMAS rule. The operators will be displayed on the screen to be performed on the digits that are enclosed in the story. At the end you will obtain an answer and the top one's will be qualified to the next round based on their accuracy on the answer.
  • ➗Round 2: It includes Housie game but it's not just a general one, the numbers will not be revealed directly, you have to identify the numbers by solving a puzzle and complete the Housie accordingly. The first completed housies will be announced as the winners🎉
